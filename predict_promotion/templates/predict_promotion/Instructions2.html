Your task

Based on the descriptions, your task is to predict:

  1. Which fraction of people recognized the illustrated word
  2. Whether the illustration is original
  3. The gender of the writer

Payment

You will receive your payment only if you complete the entire task. You receive $0.30 for participating in the task. In addition, you can receive a bonus of up to $3.00 depending on the accuracy of your predictions. To determine the bonus, we will randomly choose one out of the 36 rounds after you completed the task.

  • Your receive $1.00 each if your prediction with respect to the originality and the gender is correct in the randomly determined round.
  • For your prediction with respect to the quality, payment depends on the deviation from the true recognition rate of the illustration presented in the randomly determined round:
    • If your answer deviates by 5 or less percentage points your bonus is $1.00.
    • If your answer deviates by more than 5 and 10 or less percentage points your bonus is $0.50.
    • If your answer deviates by more than 15 percentage points your bonus is $0.
